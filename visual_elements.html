Pepperoni and Sausage Pizza

"OMG It's Amazing"

  • D
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Sausage
  1. Roll out the dough to a nice full circle. make sure to use flour on the dough so it doesnt stick.
  2. Place the sauce in the middle of the pizza, and spread desired amount.
  3. Take the desired amount of cheese and fill until pizza is lightly covered.
  4. Take a handful of pepperoni and one by one place in on the pizza in a clockwise motion.
  5. Take 2 small handfuls of sausage and place them around the pizza. Your pizza is ready to bake!!!!!!
  6. Preheat your over to 375 degree farenheit.
  7. Cook for 20 minutes or until cheeses or melted to your preference.

This is my favorite receipe because it is easy to make, and isnt very time consuming.
